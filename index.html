Cassette

A desktop app for recording and transcribing meetings. Built with Electron and designed to look like it belongs on a 1991 Macintosh.

Cassette captures system audio and microphone simultaneously, runs local transcription via Whisper, and saves everything organized by client. The UI uses Chicago and Geneva bitmap fonts with a single-window, skeuomorphic tape deck interface.

The interesting technical challenge was getting Electron 40 to render bitmap fonts without crashing Chromium's font backend. The solution was simpler than expected: stop trying to disable the new renderer and just let it work.
